Cows and Bulls

Instructions
  • You have to guess the three digit number in which each digit is distinct.
  • Based on your guess you get the number of cows and bulls.
    1. If there is a common digit and not in the correct position, then you get one cow.
    2. If there is a common digit and in the correct position, then you get one bull.
  • Start guessing by clicking on "Guess me !!".
  • Guess the correct number to get three bulls and win !
